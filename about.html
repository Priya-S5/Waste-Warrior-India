🗡️ About Waste Warriors India

Transforming waste into wisdom. One citizen, one bin, one clean street at a time.

🌍 Our Mission

Waste Warriors India is a civic-tech movement committed to revolutionizing waste management across the country. We empower citizens, train waste workers, and build digital infrastructure to ensure every gram of waste is accounted for, treated, and reused.

📈 Why It Matters

India generates over 1.7 lakh tonnes of municipal solid waste daily. Yet, only 54% is scientifically treated. The rest ends up in landfills, water bodies, or open areas—posing serious environmental and health risks. We believe every citizen is a waste generator—and therefore, a potential waste warrior.

🧠 What We Do

  • Mandatory training for citizens and waste workers
  • Decentralized monitoring via “Green Champions”
  • Geo-tagged waste reporting and tracking
  • Digital marketplace for compost kits and smart bins
  • Community clean-up drives and civic engagement

🛠️ Built With Purpose

This platform blends gamification, civic-tech, and environmental impact. From composting tutorials to real-time dashboards, every module is designed to educate, engage, and empower.
